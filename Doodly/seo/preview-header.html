Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial for Doodly?

Yes. Doodly offers a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get full access to all features during the trial period.

Can I use Doodly for commercial projects?

Commercial use requires the Enterprise plan ($69/month, billed annually). The standard plan covers personal and educational use only.

Does Doodly work on Mac and PC?

Yes — Doodly is a desktop application available for both Windows and Mac. There is no browser-based version; you must install it on your computer.

How does Doodly compare to VideoScribe or Animaker?

Doodly is the easiest to learn — most users create their first video in under 2 hours. VideoScribe has more hand styles but a steeper learning curve.
